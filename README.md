📊 Marketing Campaign Dashboard — Excel Analytics Project

An interactive marketing analytics dashboard built in Microsoft Excel, analysing the performance of 6 campaigns across 5 locations over a 5-month period (August–December 2025).

![Dashboard Preview](screenshots/dashboard.png)

---

## 📁 Project Structure

| Sheet | Description |
|---|---|
| `Campaign Metrics` | Raw data — 154 rows of daily campaign performance records |
| `Dashboard` | Summary PivotTable with conversion rates by campaign and location |
| `Campaign Performance` | Key insights on campaign-level results |
| `Location Performance` | Breakdown of performance by marketing channel |
| `Monthly Sales` | Month-by-month sales trend analysis |
| `Charts & Tables` | Supporting PivotCharts and visual summaries |

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Features Used

- **Microsoft Excel**
- PivotTables & PivotCharts
- Slicers for interactive filtering
- Conversion rate calculations (`Units Sold / Clicks`)
- Data validation and structured formatting
- Power Query

---

## 📌 Key Insights

### 🏆 Campaign Performance
- **Mt Lab Mania** was the top campaign by volume — **5,729 units sold**
- **Walter Promo** was the most efficient, achieving a **59.8% conversion rate**
- Average conversion rate across all campaigns: **43.7%**

### 📍 Location Performance
- **Car Wash Forums** had the highest conversion rate at **63.75%**
- **Fast Food Sites** drove the most units sold — **6,372 units**
- Fan Forums and Email Campaigns had the lowest conversion rates (below 38%)

### 📅 Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales peaked in **November** with **6,348 units** — a 36% increase from October
- **August** had the lowest sales at **3,226 units**
- Overall, sales nearly doubled from August to November
